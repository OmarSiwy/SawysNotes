## Intrinsic Semiconductors
Pure silicon has a crystal lattice structure. At T=0K, there are no free carriers.

$$ n_i = \sqrt{N_c N_v} e^{-E_g/2kT} $$

> [!NOTE] 
> $n_i$ is the intrinsic carrier concentration.
