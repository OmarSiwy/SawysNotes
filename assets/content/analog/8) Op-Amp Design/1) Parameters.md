Previously, we've covered Op-Amps as blackbox devices with specific parameters like:

- Open-Loop Gain
- Bandwidth
- CMRR
- Current & Voltage Offset
- Slew Rate

Now, to enhance our analysis of them, we must describe them using the extended list below:

- Gain (Open-Loop & Closed-Loop)
- Small-Signal Bandwidth
- Large-Signal Behavior
- Output Swing
- Linearity
- Noise & Offset
- Supply Rejection (PSR, Power Supply Rejection)
- Slew Rate
- Input/Output Impedance
